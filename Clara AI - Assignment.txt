Context  –  How  Clara  Answers  Works  in  the  Real  World  
What  Clara  Is  
Clara  Answers  is  an  AI-powered  voice  agent  built  using  Retell.  
It  handles  inbound  calls  for  service  trade  businesses  such  as:  
●  Fire  protection  companies  ●  Sprinkler  and  alarm  contractors  ●  Electrical  service  providers  ●  HVAC  and  facility  maintenance  companies  
These  companies  deal  with:  
●  Emergency  calls  (example:  sprinkler  leak,  fire  alarm  triggered)  ●  Non-emergency  service  requests  ●  Inspection  scheduling  ●  After-hours  routing  requirements  ●  Integration  constraints  with  systems  like  ServiceTrade  
Each  client  has  slightly  different  workflows,  escalation  paths,  and  business  rules.  
Our  challenge  is  to  configure  Clara  correctly  and  consistently,  at  scale.  
The  Client  Journey  
Stage  1  –  Demo  Call  (Exploratory  and  Value-Driven)  
The  demo  call  is  not  a  configuration  session.

●  The  client  explains  their  pain  points.  ●  They  describe  current  call  handling  problems.  ●  They  interact  with  an  example  Clara  agent.

Stage  3  –  Onboarding  Call  (Operational  Precision)  
The  onboarding  call  is  configuration-focused.  
This  is  where:  
●  Exact  business  hours  are  confirmed.  ●  Time  zones  are  finalized.  ●  Emergency  definitions  are  clearly  defined.  ●  After-hours  routing  logic  is  specified.  ●  Transfer  timeouts  are  decided.  

●  Fallback  logic  is  clarified.  ●  Integration  rules  are  confirmed.  ●  Special  constraints  are  introduced.  
Examples:  
●  “All  emergency  sprinkler  calls  must  go  directly  to  the  phone  tree.”  ●  “Non-emergency  extinguisher  calls  can  be  collected  after  hours.”  ●  “Never  create  sprinkler  jobs  in  ServiceTrade.”  ●  “If  transfer  fails  after  60  seconds,  dispatch  must  be  notified.”
